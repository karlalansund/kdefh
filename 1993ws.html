Top 5 Greatest World Series Games of All Time

#5, 1993 World Series, Game 6

Down 5-6 and on the verge of the Philadelphia Phillies forcing a game 7, Mitch "Wild Thing" Williams allowed two baserunners with Joe Carter coming up to the plate. On a 2-2 count Carter hooked a homerun around the left field pole and won 8-6. Carter hit the first walkoff homerun in a World Series clincher in 33 years. This was the Toronto Blue Jays' first World Series win in franchise history and the first World Series won by a Canadian team.
